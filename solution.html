TSBOX™ vs. Plastic Totes

Standard plastic totes solve durability—but not usability. TSBOX™ is engineered for real moving scenarios, not generic storage.

✓ TSBOX™

  • ✓Variety of specialty boxes for glass, dishes, shoes & wardrobe
  • ✓Extremely strong — made of high-density polyethylene (HDPE)
  • ✓Integrated rollers for easy, ergonomic handling
  • ✓Secure locking mechanism — contents stay safe
  • ✓GPS tracking device built-in for full accountability
  • ✓No bubble wrap, tape, or protective foam needed

✗ Common Plastic Totes

  • ✗Comes in one size — not effective for packing varied items
  • ✗Not secure and cannot be locked
  • ✗Difficult to handle and impossible to move safely
  • ✗Requires bubble wrap, tape and foam — messy and costly
  • ✗No tracking — boxes go missing with no accountability
  • ✗Generic design fails fragile and specialty items
