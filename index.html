Questions Answered

Frequently Asked Questions

How does delivery and pickup work?
+
We deliver clean, stacked TSBOX™ containers to your current address on your chosen date. When done, call us and we schedule a free pickup from your new address anywhere in our service area.
How long can I keep the boxes?
+
Standard packages are priced per week. Need more time? Extra weeks available at a flat rate — just let us know before your pickup date.
Are the boxes clean and sanitized?
+
Absolutely. Every TSBOX™ container is fully cleaned, sanitized, and inspected between every rental. You'll never receive a musty or dirty box.
What if I need more boxes than my package includes?
+
Easy — call us at 310-999-5015 or note it in the order form. We'll customize any package and deliver additional boxes if needed.
Where do you deliver?
+
We serve the greater Los Angeles area — Beverly Hills, Santa Monica, West Hollywood, Culver City, Burbank, and surrounding cities. Call to check your zip code.
What happens if a box is damaged?
+
Our boxes are built to last 80+ years, so damage is rare. Normal wear is covered — we only charge for severe damage. Just let us know at pickup.
