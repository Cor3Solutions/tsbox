The Ugly Truth

Your Cardboard Box
Is Killing the Planet

Every cardboard box you buy, use once, and throw away was once a living tree. Cardboard manufacturing consumes energy, water, and oil at enormous scale — and releases CO₂ and methane gases directly into the atmosphere.

Deforestation caused by cardboard and paper production is one of the leading contributors to greenhouse emissions on Earth — surpassing the combined output of all factories and aircraft globally.

With over 13 million U.S. households moving each year, the average 2-bedroom move alone consumes 60 cardboard boxes. That's a mountain of single-use waste generated by a single weekend.

The Solution

Single-Use Packaging
Is a Massive Liability

Moving today still relies on a broken system — buy, use once, throw away. TSBOX™ replaces that waste with durable, reusable containers that save movers money and save the planet trees.

Built from ABS or polycarbonate with an engineered lifespan of 80+ years, specialty designs for fragile items including glassware, dishes, shoes, wardrobe and TV, integrated rollers, locking mechanisms, and GPS tracking — TSBOX™ is simply a better box in every measurable way.
