The Problem

Cardboard Is Destroying Our Planet

Every move means buying boxes once and throwing them away. With over 13 million U.S. households moving annually, that’s 780 million cardboard boxes discarded every year. Cardboard is used to ship 90% of all products in the U.S. — a $34.7 billion industry built on single-use waste. Deforestation caused by cardboard production is a leading reason for greenhouse emissions — more than all factories and aircraft combined.
